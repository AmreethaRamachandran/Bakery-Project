# Combo Page Color Scheme Options

## Option 1: Warm Bakery Theme (Current + Enhanced)

- **Background**: Cream to Peach gradient
- **Section backgrounds**: Light beige cards
- **Title**: Deep brown with orange accents
- **Description**: Creates a warm, inviting bakery atmosphere

## Option 2: Fresh & Clean

- **Background**: White to Light Blue gradient
- **Section backgrounds**: Soft white with blue tint
- **Title**: Navy blue with orange accents
- **Description**: Modern, clean look with fresh feeling

## Option 3: Elegant Brown Tones

- **Background**: Cream to Light Brown gradient
- **Section backgrounds**: White cards with brown borders
- **Title**: Dark chocolate brown
- **Description**: Sophisticated, premium bakery feel

## Option 4: Vibrant & Playful

- **Background**: Light yellow to Light orange gradient
- **Section backgrounds**: White with colored accents
- **Title**: Bright orange with red tones
- **Description**: Fun, energetic, attracts attention

## Option 5: Subtle Gradient Mix

- **Background**: Alternating section colors (cream, light peach, white)
- **Section backgrounds**: Different tints per section
- **Title**: Varying between brown and orange
- **Description**: Visual variety, keeps page interesting
